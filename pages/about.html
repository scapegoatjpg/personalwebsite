Hey there! I'm Laura Longoria, a Prairie View A&M Graduate, Computer Science. Currently, I work at Heights High School in HISD, where I am a dedicated substitute for the school. I basically take over a classroom that needs a substitute to cover for the teacher. I utilize instructions left from the teacher and have students do assignments in a controlled manner. My biggest responsibility in this job is teaching the AP Computer Science Principles course in the school, which is the main reason I am currently just a dedicated substitute. I am on track in completing my alternative teaching certification through Texas Teachers of Tomorrow, and will become the official Computer Science teacher for Heights High School. With assistance from the TEALS program, we will be helping these students succeed their AP exam by creating fast paced yet understandable lesson plans to compensate for the missed instructions from the previous teacher.
A little more about myself, I spent all four years and graduated at PVAMU with Magnum Cum Laude status, cummulative GPA of 3.84. I am a transgender woman, I hope you are alright and accepting of that!
